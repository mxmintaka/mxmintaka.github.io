Modelling the kinematics of globular clusters

I update the globular cluster formation model developed by Choksi & Gnedin (2019) by adding positional and kinematic information to the modeled GCs. See Chen and Gnedin (2022) for more details.

Formation of massive star clusters in giant molecular clouds

Together with Hui Li and Mark Vogelsberger, I perform a suite of simulations to investigate the effects of initial density profiles on the evolution of star clusters in giant molecular clouds (GMCs). We find that the uniform profile follows a "hierarchical" cluster formation mode, while the steep power-law profiles show an "accretion" dominated mode. These two cluster formation modes lead to different proprieties of the most massive clusters in GMCs. See our first paper (Chen, Li, and Vogelsberger 2021) for details.
